Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DocKey clinic management software in India.

What is clinic management software? +
Clinic management software is a digital platform that helps doctors and clinic owners manage appointments, patient records, billing, prescriptions, and overall clinic operations — replacing manual registers and paper-based workflows with a streamlined, cloud-based system.
Which is the best clinic management software in India? +
The best clinic management software in India should offer appointment management, electronic medical records (EMR), billing, patient communication, and mobile accessibility through a dedicated clinic app. DocKey is designed specifically for Indian healthcare workflows with GST billing, WhatsApp integration, and vernacular support.
Can dental clinics use DocKey? +
Yes. DocKey functions as a full-featured dental clinic management software with support for dental treatment tracking, procedure scheduling, recall reminders, digital patient records, and prescription management — everything a modern dental practice needs.
What is a clinic app? +
A clinic app allows doctors and clinic staff to manage appointments, patient records, prescriptions, and patient communication from their mobile devices — anytime, anywhere. DocKey's clinic app is available for both Android and iOS.
Is DocKey suitable for small clinics? +
Absolutely. DocKey is designed for individual doctors, small single-doctor clinics, multi-specialty practices, and growing healthcare businesses across India. Plans scale with your clinic's size and needs.
Does DocKey work on mobile phones? +
Yes. DocKey has a dedicated clinic app for Android and iOS so doctors and staff can manage the entire clinic from a smartphone — including appointments, EMR access, billing, and patient communication — without needing to be at a computer.
